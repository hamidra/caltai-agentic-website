# Why post-sales onboarding breaks after the deal closes

Signed clients often do not stall because the agency lacks a checklist.

They stall because the work after payment lives across too many places: CRM notes, inboxes, Slack, docs, calendars, and someone’s memory.

## The common failure points

The first blockers usually appear around:

- missing assets
- delayed kickoff scheduling
- unclear internal handoff
- slow client approvals
- no single owner for the onboarding run

## The real issue

The problem is not tracking. Most teams already have tools that track work.

The problem is ownership. Someone still has to notice what is blocked, chase the next step, update the team, and keep momentum alive.

## What should happen instead

A post-sales onboarding run should have:

- one visible owner
- clear next action
- known blockers
- approval points
- status visibility
- follow-up until first value is reached
